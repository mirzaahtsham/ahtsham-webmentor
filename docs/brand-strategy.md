# Brand Strategy — Ahtsham Web Mentor (AWM)

## 1. Brand Identity

**Name:** Mirza Ahtsham  
**Brand Name:** Ahtsham Web Mentor (AWM)  

**Brand Type:**  
Personal Brand (Primary) + Freelance Service Provider (Secondary)

**Vision:**  
To become an authority web mentor and professional website developer serving international clients (USA, UK, UAE, Canada) while helping students grow from beginner to advanced level in web development.

---

## 2. Positioning

### For Students:
A web mentor helping beginners and intermediate learners build real-world skills, create portfolios, and solve practical development problems.

### For Clients:
A professional WordPress & Shopify developer specializing in:
- Business website development
- Website issue fixing & recovery
- Performance optimization
- SEO integrations
- Secure migrations and maintenance

### Combined Positioning:
**Web Mentor & Professional Website Developer for Businesses and Learners**

---

## 3. Target Audience

### A. Students

- People who want to start freelancing or tech careers
- Students struggling with real-world problems

### B. Clients (Primary Markets)
- USA 🇺🇸
